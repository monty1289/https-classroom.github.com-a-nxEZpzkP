WoodMallets Location

Wood Mallets is located in Hawkes Bay, New Zealand although orders can be delivered anywhere in the world usually within a few days. The majority of our orders are placed via the internet and exported, although local customers are welcome to visit most days by appointment.

Directions from Otane to Wood Mallets

Otane is just off the main road, 25 minutes South of Hastings and 10 minutes North of Waipukurau. Drive East straight through the township of Otane for about 2km until you come to a T Junction signposted Elsthorpe to the left and Racecourse Road to the right.

After 200 metres on Elsthorpe Road, Turn right on to Tod Road. Drive for 2.8 km's, bearing right on Tod Road (where Evans Roads joins from the left). We're the 4th property on the right on Tod Road. It’s a large brown and white homestead called “The Park” with several farm buildings. Rapid # 280.
